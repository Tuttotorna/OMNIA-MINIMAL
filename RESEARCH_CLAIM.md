# Research Claim

## Minimal claim

    Correctness is not structural stability.

An output may be surface-correct while becoming structurally fragile under independent transformations.

OMNIA-MINIMAL demonstrates this gap in the smallest reproducible form.

---

## What is being measured

The demo measures whether outputs remain structurally compatible across transformed versions of the same underlying task.

The measurement is post-hoc.

The output already exists.

OMNIA does not generate the answer.

OMNIA measures the behavior of the answer under transformation.

---

## What is not being claimed

This repository does not claim that OMNIA proves truth.

It does not claim that stability equals semantic correctness.

It does not claim that instability equals falsehood.

It does not claim universal validity.

It demonstrates a narrower, falsifiable point:

    structural instability can be detected even when surface correctness appears to pass
