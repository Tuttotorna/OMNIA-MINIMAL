# Boundaries

OMNIA-MINIMAL does not prove semantic truth.

It does not decide whether an answer should be trusted.

It does not replace domain validation.

It measures structural behavior under transformation.

The measured signal may support a downstream decision, but the decision is external to OMNIA.

    measurement != inference != decision

---

## Core boundary

A stable structure is not automatically true.

An unstable structure is not automatically false.

Stability is a signal.

Not a verdict.

---

## What the demo can show

The demo can show that different transformed cases produce different structural stability signals.

It can show a gap between surface correctness and structural stability.

It can show that a measurement layer can exist after generation.

---

## What the demo cannot show

The demo cannot prove that OMNIA is universally valid.

It cannot prove that a model understands a task.

It cannot prove semantic truth.

It cannot replace external review.

It cannot replace scientific validation.
